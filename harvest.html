Common questions

Shop and store with confidence

Does seasonal produce always mean local?

No. Seasonal describes natural growing cycles; local describes distance. A product may be one, both or neither.

Is imperfect produce lower quality?

Cosmetic variation is normal. Judge freshness by aroma, firmness and signs of damage or spoilage.

How can I reduce food waste?

Plan a few flexible meals, store produce correctly, use delicate items first and freeze suitable extras.
