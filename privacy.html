Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains how Harvest Table Daily collects, uses and protects information when you browse, subscribe or contact us.

1. Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, messages and subscription choices. With consent, technical data may include visited pages, device type, approximate location, referral source and cookie identifiers.

2. How we use information

Information helps us respond to enquiries, provide requested updates, improve content, secure the website and meet legal obligations. Marketing is sent only where permitted, with an unsubscribe option.

3. Cookies and analytics

Optional analytics storage remains disabled until consent where required. Necessary local storage may remember your cookie choice.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Necessary information may be shared with hosting, email, analytics and professional providers under appropriate obligations, or when legally required.

5. Retention and security

We retain information only as long as needed for its purpose and legal duties and use reasonable safeguards. No online system can guarantee complete security.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection or withdrawal of consent by contacting privacy@harvesttabledaily.com.

7. Children

The site is intended for a general adult audience and does not knowingly collect information from children below the applicable minimum age.

8. Contact

Privacy questions may be sent to privacy@harvesttabledaily.com. Identity verification may be required.
