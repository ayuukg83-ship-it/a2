Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains how Harvest Table Daily uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small browser files. Similar technologies such as local storage may remember choices or help measure use.

2. Necessary technologies

A necessary preference may remember your cookie selection so the banner does not appear on every page.

3. Analytics

If enabled, analytics may help measure popular pages, devices, errors and referral sources. Optional analytics should activate only after consent where required.

4. Your choices

Select Accept all for optional analytics or Necessary only to keep them disabled. Clear site data in your browser to reset the choice.

5. Third parties

Embedded content, analytics and linked services may use technologies under their own privacy policies.

6. Contact

Cookie questions may be sent to privacy@harvesttabledaily.com.
